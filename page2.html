ABOUT US

We are a proudly South African business that occupies a covered workshop premises of 3000m2 in Prospecton, Durban which is where we have been based for the past 29 years and have evolved into a multifaceted Engineering Business, with a level 1 BBBEE Rating.

We pride ourselves in the successful completion of the many projects undertaken by the business and continually strive to be better, having employed suitably skilled personnel that ultimately become value adding to you, our client.
